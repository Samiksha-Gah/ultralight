Dance Studio Poem

In a studio's grace,
Gentle tapping paint the air,
Brute elegance takes form.

In the mirrored movements,
Reflections blur truth and tale,
Secrets softly kept.

In the hastening rhythm,
Sideways glances, tension brews,
Yet the beat rings louds.

Yet exhaustion rests,
In the quiet moments, still,
Fuel for the next flight.

Amidst the melody,
Stories unfold, souls entwine,
In a dance of life.
