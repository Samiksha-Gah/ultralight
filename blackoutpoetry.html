vis 208
vis 208 is clean
scrubbed and washed but not
in the way of chemicals and stark white
but how an apron is lightly spattered with
fragrant constellations of paint
vis 208 is the soft beige of crackling parchment
and the windows twice my height
free just enough to hear
the humming of cars and pitter of rain
i learn how to do more
with less
around an empty void of parchment paper
vis 208 is the thoughtful key clicks
that are melodious enough to
fill it
