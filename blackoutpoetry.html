sam's room is clean
scrubbed and washed but not
in the way of chemicals and stark white
but how an apron is lightly spattered with
fragrant constellations of paint
her room is the soft hums of her laptop
and scratching of her pencil
free just enough to hear
the pitter of rain
around her an empty void of wood
her room is the thoughtful key clicks
that are melodious enough to
fill it
